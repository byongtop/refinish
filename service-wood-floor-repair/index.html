Our Floor Repair Philosophy

Fast, Cost-Effective, and Eco-Friendly

Unlike traditional floor replacement, our repair method avoids buying new boards or disrupting existing flooring. Repairs are completed in just a few hours to half a day. No transportation, cutting, or installation is needed, avoiding batch color mismatches while saving time and money.

Our Technical Advantage

Precise Paint Matching, Hand-Painted Wood Grain, Original Shine Restored

We mix multiple paints to match your original floor color and hand-paint wood grain for a seamless finish. The shine is restored to match the original surface. Unlike repairs using wax or pigments, we use professional-grade paint and offer a warranty for lasting results.

What We Can Fix

Scratches, Dents, Mold, Peeling Paint—We Handle It All

Our repair service works on all types of wooden flooring, from home and office floors to high-end hardwood. Common damages we repair include:

  • Minor or deep scratches
  • Dents and small depressions
  • Mold or water damage
  • Peeling paint or discoloration

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the repaired area match my floor’s original color?

Using precise paint matching and hand-painted wood grain, the repaired section blends seamlessly with the original flooring.

How long will the repair last?

We use high-quality paints and professional techniques. Under normal use, results are long-lasting and come with a warranty.

Do I need to clear the entire room for repairs?

No, repairs are quick and localized, so daily life is minimally affected.

Is floor repair inferior to replacement?

Not at all. Our hand-crafted repair restores your floor to the same beauty and shine as new boards.

Can all types of damage be repaired?

We can fix common scratches, dents, mold, and peeling paint. Severe structural damage can be evaluated with a professional plan.
